Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

PRO P2P Terminal ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, encrypted password hash (handled by Firebase Authentication).
  • Trade data: order amounts, timestamps, counterparty IDs, chat messages, payment confirmations you upload.
  • Wallet data: USDT balance, deposit/withdrawal history, blockchain addresses you provide.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device info (used for fraud prevention).

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not ask for or store passwords/2FA codes for any third-party exchange (Binance, WazirX, etc.).
  • We do not collect your bank account password, UPI PIN, or card CVV.
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the P2P trading service and process escrow.
  • To resolve disputes between buyers and sellers.
  • To send transactional emails (deposit/withdraw/trade notifications) via EmailJS.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse and money-laundering.
  • To comply with Indian legal obligations.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Account and trade data is stored on Google Firebase Realtime Database. Email notifications are sent through EmailJS. Both providers are bound by their own security and privacy commitments.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies / localStorage to keep you logged in and remember your active trade. No advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except: (a) our service providers (Firebase, EmailJS), (b) when required by law or court order, (c) to prevent fraud.

7. Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, correction of inaccuracies, or account deletion by contacting t.me/prop2p10 or support@prop2p.in.

8. Security

HTTPS is enforced site-wide. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase. Despite reasonable safeguards, no internet service is 100% secure — please use a strong unique password.

9. Children

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.

11. Contact

Privacy questions: support@prop2p.in or t.me/prop2p10.
