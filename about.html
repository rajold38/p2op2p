How long does INR settlement take?
Average INR release time is under 2 minutes via UPI/IMPS once the seller confirms payment. Escrow USDT release is instant after that.

Biexc is fee-light for takers and free for makers during promo cycles. The exact fee for your trade is shown live on the terminal before you confirm.

No. Biexc is a fully independent Indian P2P platform. We are not affiliated with Binance or any centralized exchange.
