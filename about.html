About PRO P2P

PRO P2P Terminal is an independent peer-to-peer (P2P) cryptocurrency trading platform that helps verified users in India buy and sell USDT (Tether) for Indian Rupees (INR) through escrow-protected trades.

Our Mission

To make P2P stablecoin trading in India safe, transparent and instant — with real human support, verified merchants and a clean, distraction-free interface.

How It Works

  • Users sign up with email and verify their identity via Firebase Authentication.
  • Buyers and sellers post orders. PRO P2P holds USDT in escrow until both parties confirm the trade.
  • INR payments are settled directly between users via UPI/IMPS — we never hold fiat money.
  • All chats and trade events are recorded for dispute resolution.

Security

  • HTTPS enforced site-wide with HSTS.
  • Authentication via Google Firebase.
  • Escrow system prevents double-spend or merchant fraud.
  • 24×7 human support on Telegram.
